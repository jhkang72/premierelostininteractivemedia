The Film: Lost in Interactive Media

A fast-paced, visually engaging short film that follows a student’s chaotic journey through the Interactive Media Center after waking up late for class. Along the way, she gets sidetracked by the captivating creative projects around her—until a timely reminder snaps her back to reality. A playful exploration of distraction, curiosity, and the immersive pull of media environments.
